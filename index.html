Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Pandas

  • Panda's have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Panda's eyes are different to normal bears.
  • Panda cubs are well protected in their first month.

Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff. She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903.
